About the author

Coral Compact Disc Lastname (born unfortunately) has written for English class, journalism class, and probably a few comedy bits. It lives in Colorado and is known for many works such as The Giver Essay, The Incomprehensible Tale of a Man Named Jeremy, The Best Story Ever Written, and of course, Peppa Packet and Peppa Packet Reprise.

Lastname never found itself with more than a C in English. As famed journalist Neo once wrote, “Lastname lives on a day-to-day basis, waking up and seeing where the day takes it. Sometimes it’s with its friends to their favorite hangout spot — a concrete tunnel.”

Lastname’s work is plagued with a sense of irony overshadowing the goals of its papers. The reason for this is not public knowledge. Apparently, the neurologist said they don’t treat people under 18.

When we asked its associate, Dane Marks, for comment, he said: “The fridge was running, and it ran too fast. It got away, and it all went downhill from there.” He added, “And they ran out of Peppa.”

When we reached out to Lastname directly for comment, it said: “Dude, fuck are you on about my writing is totally bitchin’ dude.”
