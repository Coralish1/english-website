Peppa packet

Peppa packet
Humble beginnings as a peppercorn
Toasted, roasted, and grounded up
Maybe you’ll be ripped and poured
Maybe you’ll just end up in Dane’s door
Peppa packet
Tossed in a fast-food bag
Tossed into the trash
Maybe in an airline meal you’ll soar the sky
Maybe you’re still above Dane’s window way up high
Peppa packet
With salt, you’ll make the perfect pair
I love you peppa

Cosco Mission Statement and Code of Ethics
